Privacy Policy — AquaFeed

AquaFeed collects limited personal information necessary to provide SMS alert functionality, including a user’s mobile phone number and message delivery records. This information is used only to send operational notifications related to AquaFeed feeder errors, system failures, connectivity issues, and other important service alerts.

AquaFeed does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. User information is shared only with service providers required to deliver SMS messages, such as telecommunications carriers and messaging platforms.

Users may opt out of SMS alerts at any time by replying STOP to any message or by disabling SMS notifications within the AquaFeed software.

AquaFeed uses reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect user information. Information is retained only as long as needed to provide the service and maintain basic delivery records.
